# Doom-Scroll Blocker

Doom-Scroll Blocker is a browser extension that blocks users from Doom Scrolling major social media sites while allowing them to view specific posts (such as linked from a chat app).

## Blocked Sites

Feed pages and homepages are blocked. Direct links to specific posts, videos, or content remain accessible.

| Site | Blocked | Allowed |
|------|---------|---------|
| Reddit | Homepage, subreddit feeds | Individual posts (`/r/*/comments/*`) |
| Twitter / X | Home feed | Individual tweets (`/*/status/*`) |
| Instagram | Homepage feed | Posts (`/p/*`), Reels (`/reel/*`) |
| TikTok | For You Page | Individual videos (`/@*/video/*`) |
| Facebook | News feed | — |
| YouTube | Homepage, Shorts feed | Watch URLs (`/watch*`) |
| LinkedIn | News feed | — |
| Threads | Home feed | Individual posts (`/t/*`) |
| Bluesky | Home feed | Individual posts (`/profile/*/post/*`) |
| Pinterest | All pages | — |
